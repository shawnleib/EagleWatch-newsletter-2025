ü¶Ö Telemetry Study Launched!

This spring we fulfilled a long-held dream of mine to launch a new telemetry tracking study with Florida's Bald Eagles! The previous telemetry studies were done 15-20 years ago and Florida's landscape has changed dramatically during that time--more people, more roads, and the conversion of natural areas to developed. How may those earlier findings have changed over time as our eagles learn to adapt to increasing urbanization? This knowledge will help us stay on the forefront of understanding population parameters and pressures in order to better inform conservation management decisions to ensure a bright future for Florida's Bald Eagles.
